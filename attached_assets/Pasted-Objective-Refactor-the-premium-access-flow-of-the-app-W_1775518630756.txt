Objective:
Refactor the premium access flow of the app WITHOUT changing any existing free plan limits or core functionality.

Current problem:
Users receive a 14-day premium trial automatically upon signup, which removes urgency and reduces conversion.

Goal:
Introduce a controlled activation flow where users experience value first, hit a natural limit, and THEN are prompted to activate the trial.

Requirements:

1. Remove automatic trial activation on signup
- New users should start in FREE mode
- Do NOT grant premium access immediately

2. Introduce usage-based trigger for trial
- After the user performs 2–3 meaningful actions (e.g. answering questions, writing reflections, or interacting with content), trigger a paywall modal

Trigger examples:
- After answering 3 questions
- After attempting to access a locked question
- After trying to continue deeper interaction

3. Paywall modal behavior
Display a modal with:

Title:
"Você está começando a se aprofundar"

Description:
"Para continuar explorando perguntas mais profundas e evoluir na sua jornada, desbloqueie 14 dias grátis"

CTA:
"Ativar 14 dias grátis"

Important:
- NO credit card required
- Trial only starts after user clicks this CTA

4. Maintain current free plan limitations
- Do NOT change:
  - diary limits
  - question limits
  - journey access rules

5. Add contextual soft prompts (non-blocking)
At key moments, show subtle prompts:

Examples:
- After a strong question:
  "Essa foi só uma das perguntas. Existem outras que podem te fazer pensar diferente."

- After multiple interactions:
  "Você já começou algo importante aqui."

These should NOT block usage, only guide toward perceived value

6. End-of-trial conversion trigger
When trial is about to end (24h before), show:

Title:
"Seu acesso premium está acabando"

Description:
"Você já refletiu X vezes e avançou na sua jornada. Continue sua evolução."

CTA:
"Continuar com acesso completo"

7. Track events (important)
Add tracking for:
- trial_started
- paywall_viewed
- paywall_clicked
- trial_ended
- conversion_to_paid

Constraints:
- Do NOT break existing flows
- Do NOT remove features
- Only modify access logic and UI prompts

Outcome:
Users should:
1. Experience value first
2. Hit a natural limit
3. Choose to activate trial
4. Feel progress
5. Be prompted to convert
